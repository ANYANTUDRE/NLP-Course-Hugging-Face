## ğŸ§© Named entity recognition (NER)

##### Example ğŸ‘¾


```
  'score': 0.9981694,
  'word': 'Sylvain',
  'start': 11,

  'score': 0.9796019,
  'word': 'Hugging Face',
  'start': 33,
  'end': 45},

  'score': 0.9932106,
```

Here the model correctly identified that Sylvain is a **person (PER)**, Hugging Face an **organization (ORG)**, and Brooklyn a **location (LOC)**.
